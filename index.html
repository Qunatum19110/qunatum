Computer

Computer is a word derived from the word “compute” which means to calculate. It is a fast-calculating device which receives input, process data, and gives us information as output. It is used in various field such as

  1. Homes
  2. Offices and business
  3. At educational and hospital sectors
Nowadays the computer has become crucial for our life. Almost every person depends on gadgets to run effectively for one reason or another if they do not own one. People who have a bank account also have a bank card in which all their information and credit are saved. All their bank details are saved or kept on a computer system. If this computer was failed to the bank would have all the informationInformation kept on a backup system. In this way their information is saved throughout their life.
